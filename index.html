Focaccia with Caramelized Onions,
Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your taste buds with this festive focaccia appetitizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cramberris, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flor
  • 1 1/2 teaspons instant yeast
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespon olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bowl,or in a stand mixer with a paddle attacment,mix thogether all the dough ingredients until the flour is fulli absorbed.
